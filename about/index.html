About

For nearly two decades I have been running tabletop RPGs like D&D. Ever since a middle school teacher introduced me to 2nd edition Dungeons and Dragons, I was hooked. In the back of history class I’d run a small game for a group of anti-authority friends. We smashed down doors, explored dungeons, fought orcs, and found treasure. Soon, I was off to college and bigger groups and long lasting campaigns.

I was inspired by Hank at Runehammer to share what I’ve been working on with the world. Sword and Tower Games is the incarnation of years of gameplay as a gamemaster and board game enthusiast. I’m creating content that is easy to take with you and easy to run. Thanks for stopping by!
